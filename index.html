About

Who we are

It is a national, independent, non-profit organization registered with the Humanitarian Aid Commission (HAC). The organization works on training and qualifying active youth in the field of volunteer work to enhance their practical capabilities and equip them with the necessary skills to manage and develop various types and areas of volunteer projects.

Volunteer Making Course (VMC)
VMC = Volunteer Making Course
This is a training program aimed at enhancing the knowledge and skills of volunteers to enable them to manage and develop volunteer programs and projects professionally.
